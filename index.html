About the Association

The Montague Police Association (MPA) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that supports the members of the Montague Police Department and related public safety initiatives. The Association works to promote officer wellness, professional development, and positive community engagement.

Our Mission

Officer Wellness

Support the physical and mental wellness of our officers through training, fitness, and health-related initiatives.

Community Support

Strengthen relationships between law enforcement and the community we serve through outreach and charitable programs.

Professional Development

Encourage continued education, training, and professional growth for public safety personnel.

Donations & Fundraising

The Montague Police Association relies on the generosity of local businesses, organizations, and community members. Donations directly support approved association programs and projects, including officer wellness and community initiatives.

Tax Information:
The Montague Police Association is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Contributions may be tax-deductible as allowed by law. Tax receipts are available upon request.

For donation inquiries or to arrange a contribution, please contact us directly.
